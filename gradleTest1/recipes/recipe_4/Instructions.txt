Instructions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the sliced chicken and cook until browned and fully cooked, about 5-6 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sliced bell pepper. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until the bell pepper is slightly softened.
4. Pour in the can of diced tomatoes, including the juice, and bring to a simmer.
5. Stir in the milk and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Add the cooked pasta and cooked chicken back into the skillet, stirring well to coat everything in the creamy tomato sauce.
7. Continue to cook for a few minutes until everything is heated through.
8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired and serve hot.
