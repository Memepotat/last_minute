
Tomato and Cucumber Salad
(Difficulty: Easy)
Time estimate: 15 minutes

Ingredients:
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 1/4 cup diced bell pepper
- 1/4 cup diced red onion
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, sliced cucumber, diced bell pepper, diced red onion, and chopped fresh parsley.
2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and lemon juice.
4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.
5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later use.
