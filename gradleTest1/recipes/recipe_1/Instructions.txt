Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cooked pasta, diced tomatoes, sliced bell pepper, shredded cheese, milk, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
3. Transfer the mixture into a baking dish and spread evenly.
4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
5. Let it cool for a few minutes, then serve hot.
