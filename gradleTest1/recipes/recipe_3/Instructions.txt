Instructions:
1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Set aside.
2. Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
3. Add the diced bell pepper and saut√© for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.
4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are scrambled and cooked to your desired consistency.
5. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the scrambled eggs and season with salt and pepper.
6. Cook for another minute until the cheese is melted.
7. Serve hot with toast or alongside a salad.
