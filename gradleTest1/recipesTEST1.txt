===\n\nChicken Pasta Bake\n(Difficulty: Easy)\nTime estimate: 45 minutes\n\nIngredients:\n- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded\n- 2 cups cooked pasta\n- 1 can diced tomatoes\n- 1 bell pepper, sliced\n- 1 cup shredded cheese\n- 1/2 cup milk\n- Salt and pepper to taste\n\nInstructions:\n1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).\n2. In a large bowl, combine the shredded chicken, cooked pasta, diced tomatoes, sliced bell pepper, shredded cheese, milk, salt, and pepper. Mix well.\n3. Transfer the mixture into a baking dish and spread evenly.\n4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.\n5. Let it cool for a few minutes, then serve hot.\n\n===\n\nTomato and Cucumber Salad\n(Difficulty: Easy)\nTime estimate: 15 minutes\n\nIngredients:\n- 2 tomatoes, diced\n- 1 cucumber, sliced\n- 1/4 cup diced bell pepper\n- 1/4 cup diced red onion\n- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley\n- Salt and pepper to taste\n- 2 tablespoons olive oil\n- 1 tablespoon lemon juice\n\nInstructions:\n1. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, sliced cucumber, diced bell pepper, diced red onion, and chopped fresh parsley.\n2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.\n3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and lemon juice.\n4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all the ingredients.\n5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for later use.\n\n===\n\nCheesy Scrambled Eggs with Peppers\n(Difficulty: Easy)\nTime estimate: 10 minutes\n\nIngredients:\n- 4 eggs\n- 1/4 cup milk\n- 1/2 bell pepper, diced\n- 1/4 cup shredded cheese\n- Salt and pepper to taste\n- 1 tablespoon butter\n\nInstructions:\n1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. Set aside.\n2. Heat the butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.\n3. Add the diced bell pepper and sauté for 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.\n4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are scrambled and cooked to your desired consistency.\n5. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the scrambled eggs and season with salt and pepper.\n6. Cook for another minute until the cheese is melted.\n7. Serve hot with toast or alongside a salad.\n\n===\n\nCreamy Chicken and Tomato Pasta\n(Difficulty: Medium)\nTime estimate: 30 minutes\n\nIngredients:\n- 2 chicken breasts, sliced\n- 2 cups cooked pasta\n- 1 can diced tomatoes\n- 1/2 bell pepper, sliced\n- 1/4 cup milk\n- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese\n- 3 cloves garlic, minced\n- 2 tablespoons olive oil\n- Salt and pepper to taste\n- Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)\n\nInstructions:\n1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.\n2. Add the sliced chicken and cook until browned and fully cooked, about 5-6 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.\n3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and sliced bell pepper. Sauté for 2-3 minutes until the bell pepper is slightly softened.\n4. Pour in the can of diced tomatoes, including the juice, and bring to a simmer.\n5. Stir in the milk and grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.\n6. Add the cooked pasta and cooked chicken back into the skillet, stirring well to coat everything in the creamy tomato sauce.\n7. Continue to cook for a few minutes until everything is heated through.\n8. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired and serve hot.\n\n===\n\nStuffed Bell Peppers with Chicken and Cheese\n(Difficulty: Hard)\nTime estimate: 60 minutes\n\nIngredients:\n- 4 large bell peppers\n- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded\n- 1 cup cooked pasta\n- 1 cup diced tomatoes\n- 1 cup shredded cheese (such as mozzarella or cheddar)\n- 1/4 cup milk\n- 1/4 cup diced onion\n- 2 cloves garlic, minced\n- 2 tablespoons olive oil\n- Salt and pepper to taste\n\nInstructions:\n1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and prepare a baking dish.\n2. Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes. Set aside.\n3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened and fragrant.\n4. Add the shredded chicken, cooked pasta, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, milk, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir well to combine.\n5. Fill each bell pepper with the chicken and pasta mixture, pressing it down gently to fill the peppers completely.\n6. Place the stuffed bell peppers in the prepared baking dish.\n7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the peppers are tender and the filling is heated through.\n8. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.
