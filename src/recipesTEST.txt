Roasted Vegetable Pasta Salad 
Easy 
Time estimate: 30 minutes 
Ingredients: 
- 1 carrot, sliced 
- 1 potato, diced 
- 1 bell pepper, sliced 
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes 
- 1/4 cup olive oil 
- Salt and pepper to taste 
- 8 oz pasta 
- 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled 

Instructions: 
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). 
2. In a baking sheet, toss the carrots, potatoes, bell pepper, and cherry tomatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. 
3. Roast the vegetables in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly charred. 
4. In a separate pot, cook the pasta according to package instructions. 
5. Drain the cooked pasta and rinse with cold water to cool it down. 
6. In a large bowl, combine the roasted vegetables, cooked pasta, and crumbled feta cheese. 
7. Toss everything together gently until well combined. 
8. Serve chilled and enjoy!

Chicken and Vegetable Stir-fry 
Medium 
Time estimate: 25 minutes 
Ingredients: 
- 1 chicken breast, sliced 
- 1 carrot, julienned 
- 1 bell pepper, sliced 
- 1 cup green beans, trimmed 
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce 
- 1 tablespoon honey 
- 1 garlic clove, minced 
- 1 teaspoon grated ginger 
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil 
- Salt and pepper to taste 
- Cooked rice or noodles, for serving 

Instructions: 
1. In a small bowl, mix together the soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, and grated ginger. Set aside. 
2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. 
3. Add the sliced chicken breast to the skillet and cook until browned and cooked through. 
4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside. 
5. In the same skillet, add another tablespoon of vegetable oil. 
6. Add the julienned carrots, sliced bell pepper, and trimmed green beans to the skillet. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender. 
7. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and pour the soy sauce mixture over the chicken and vegetables. 
8. Stir-fry everything together for another 2-3 minutes until well coated and heated through. 
9. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 
10. Serve the chicken and vegetable stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles.

Tomato and Feta Cheese Omelette 
Easy 
Time estimate: 15 minutes 
Ingredients: 
- 3 eggs 
- 1/4 cup milk 
- 1 tomato, diced 
- 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese 
- Salt and pepper to taste 
- Fresh herbs (optional) 

Instructions: 
1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk until well combined. 
2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly coat it with cooking spray or butter. 
3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it cook for a minute or two until the edges start to set. 
4. Sprinkle the diced tomato and crumbled feta cheese evenly over one-half of the omelette. 
5. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh herbs if desired. 
6. Using a spatula, carefully flip the other half of the omelette over the filling, creating a half-moon shape. 
7. Cook for another minute or until the omelette is fully cooked through. 
8. Slide the omelette onto a plate, cut it in half if desired, and serve hot.

Creamy Potato and Bean Soup 
Medium 
Time estimate: 45 minutes 
Ingredients: 
- 2 potatoes, peeled and diced 
- 1 carrot, diced 
- 1 cup cooked beans 
- 1 onion, diced 
- 2 cloves garlic, minced 
- 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth 
- 1 cup milk 
- 2 tablespoons butter 
- Salt and pepper to taste 
- Fresh parsley for garnish 

Instructions: 
1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. 
2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot and sauté until the onion is translucent. 
3. Add the diced potatoes and carrots to the pot and cook for a few minutes, stirring occasionally. 
4. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a boil. 
5. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the potatoes and carrots are tender. 
6. Using a blender or immersion blender, puree the soup until smooth. 
7. Return the pureed soup to the pot and stir in the cooked beans and milk. 
8. Simmer for another 5-10 minutes to heat through. 
9. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 
10. Serve the creamy potato and bean soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley.

Mediterranean Chicken Salad 
Easy 
Time estimate: 20 minutes 
Ingredients: 
- 1 chicken breast, cooked and diced 
- 1 carrot, shredded 
- 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced 
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved 
- 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled 
- 2 tablespoons olive oil 
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice 
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley or basil) 
- Salt and pepper to taste 

Instructions: 
1. In a large bowl, combine the diced chicken breast, shredded carrot, thinly sliced bell pepper, halved cherry tomatoes, and crumbled feta cheese. 
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, chopped fresh herbs, salt, and pepper. 
3. Pour the dressing over the chicken and vegetable mixture and toss everything together until well coated. 
4. Serve the Mediterranean chicken salad chilled, and enjoy!
