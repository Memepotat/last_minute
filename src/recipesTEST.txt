Cheesy Chicken Tomato Pasta
(Easy)
Time estimate: 30 minutes
Ingredients:
- 8 oz pasta
- 1 chicken breast, cooked and shredded
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C).
3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, shredded chicken, cherry tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
4. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish and spread it evenly.
5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Cucumber Tomato Salad
(Easy)
Time estimate: 10 minutes
Ingredients:
- 2 cucumbers, sliced
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced cucumbers, diced tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, chopped fresh dill, salt, and pepper.
3. Pour the dressing over the cucumber and tomato mixture. Toss well to coat.
4. Let the salad sit for about 5 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
5. Serve chilled as a refreshing side dish.

Chicken and Tomato Stir-Fry
(Medium)
Time estimate: 25 minutes
Ingredients:
- 1 chicken breast, thinly sliced
- 2 tomatoes, diced
- 1/2 onion, sliced
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 1/4 cup water
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and water. Set aside.
2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
3. Add the chicken slices to the hot oil and stir-fry until cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
4. In the same skillet, add the sliced onion and minced garlic. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, until fragrant.
5. Add the diced tomatoes to the skillet and cook for another 2-3 minutes until slightly softened.
6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
7. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
9. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

Cheesy Chicken Tomato Egg Bake
(Medium)
Time estimate: 45 minutes
Ingredients:
- 4 cups cooked pasta
- 2 chicken breasts, cooked and shredded
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 375째F (190째C).
2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, shredded chicken, cherry tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, chopped fresh parsley, salt, and pepper.
3. Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish and spread it evenly.
4. Create small wells in the mixture and crack an egg into each well.
5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are cooked to your liking and the cheese is melted and golden.
6. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
7. Garnish with additional chopped parsley if desired.
8. Enjoy this delicious and filling egg bake!

Chicken and Cucumber Tomato Wraps
(Easy)
Time estimate: 15 minutes
Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts, cooked and sliced
- 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
- 2 tomatoes, sliced
- 4 large tortillas
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a small bowl, combine the mayonnaise and Dijon mustard. Stir well to combine.
2. Lay the tortillas flat on a clean surface.
3. Spread a thin layer of the mayonnaise-Dijon mixture onto each tortilla.
4. Divide the sliced chicken, cucumber slices, and tomato slices evenly among the tortillas.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Roll up the tortillas tightly to form wraps.
7. Cut each wrap in half diagonally and serve immediately.
8. These wraps make a great lunch or snack!
