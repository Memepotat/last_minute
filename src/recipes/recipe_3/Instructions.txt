Instructions: 
1. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. 
2. Add the diced onion and minced garlic to the pot and saut√© until the onion is translucent. 
3. Add the diced potatoes and carrots to the pot and cook for a few minutes, stirring occasionally. 
4. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a boil. 
5. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the potatoes and carrots are tender. 
6. Using a blender or immersion blender, puree the soup until smooth. 
7. Return the pureed soup to the pot and stir in the cooked beans and milk. 
8. Simmer for another 5-10 minutes to heat through. 
9. Season with salt and pepper to taste. 
10. Serve the creamy potato and bean soup hot, garnished with fresh parsley.
