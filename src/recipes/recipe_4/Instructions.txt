Instructions: 
1. In a large bowl, combine the diced chicken breast, shredded carrot, thinly sliced bell pepper, halved cherry tomatoes, and crumbled feta cheese. 
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, chopped fresh herbs, salt, and pepper. 
3. Pour the dressing over the chicken and vegetable mixture and toss everything together until well coated. 
4. Serve the Mediterranean chicken salad chilled, and enjoy!
