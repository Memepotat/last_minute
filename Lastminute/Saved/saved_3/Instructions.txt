Instructions: 
1. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper. 
2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the sliced onion and diced bell pepper. Cook until softened. 
3. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet, spreading them evenly. 
4. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the eggs. 
5. Cook until the omelette is set and the cheese is melted, around 4-5 minutes. 
6. Fold the omelette in half and transfer it to a plate. 
7. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy!
