Apple Crumble
Medium
Time estimate: 45 minutes
Ingredients: 
- 4 apples, peeled and sliced
- 1 cup oats
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup butter, softened
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions: 
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a mixing bowl, combine the oats, flour, brown sugar, butter, and cinnamon. Mix until crumbly.
3. Place the sliced apples in a baking dish and sprinkle the crumble mixture evenly over the top.
4. Bake for about 30 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the crumble is golden brown.
5. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
