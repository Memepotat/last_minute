Chicken Paprika
Easy
Time estimate: 30 minutes
Ingredients: 
- 2 chicken breasts
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions: 
1. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and paprika.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Cook chicken breasts for about 4-5 minutes per side until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
4. In the same skillet, add diced onions and minced garlic. Saute for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
5. Stir in tomato sauce and bring it to a simmer.
6. Return the chicken breasts to the skillet, covering them with the tomato sauce. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
7. Serve hot with your choice of side dish.
