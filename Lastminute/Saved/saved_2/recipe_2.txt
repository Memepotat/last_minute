Creamy Carrot Pasta 
Medium 
Time Estimate: 30 minutes 
Ingredients: 
- 8 ounces pasta
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 carrots, grated
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup shredded cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Paprika for garnish

Instructions: 
1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions and grated carrots. Saut√© until the vegetables are softened.
3. Pour in the milk and bring to a simmer. Stir in the shredded cheese until melted and smooth.
4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until well coated with the creamy carrot sauce.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve hot, garnished with a sprinkle of paprika.
