Yogurt Pancakes with Caramelized Onions
Difficulty: Easy
Time estimate: 30 minutes
Ingredients: 
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion, thinly sliced
- Olive oil for cooking

Instructions: 
1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the yogurt and eggs until well combined.
3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Do not overmix.
4. Let the batter rest for about 10 minutes.
5. In a skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat.
6. Add the thinly sliced onions and cook until caramelized, stirring occasionally.
7. Grease the skillet and drop spoonfuls of the pancake batter onto it.
8. Cook until the edges are set and bubbles form on the surface.
9. Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until golden.
10. Serve the pancakes topped with caramelized onions.
